# Museli

Adapted from Alton Brown's Overnight Coconut Oats

## Ingredients
* 60g old-fashion rolled oats
* 45g dried fruit (cranberries, raisens, apple, dates ...)
* 3g of chia seeds
* 5g flaxseed meal
* pinch of salt
* pinch of cinamon
* 225g whole milk

## Directions
 1. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl
 2. Let it sit overnight in the fridge
