# Kohlrabi Pancakes

## Ingredients

 - 1 medium kohlrabi, grated
 - 1 potatoe, grated
 - 1 egg
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 2 tsp flour
 - Ghee for frying

## Directions

1. Beat egg
1. Add flour and salt
1. Mix in kohlrabi and pancake
1. Fry in ghee and olive oil at 5.5 for about 5 minutes per side.
1. Place on paper towel lined plate
